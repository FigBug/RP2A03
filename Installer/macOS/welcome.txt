RP2A03 by SocaLabs
===================

This installer places the RP2A03 plug-in in your system audio plug-in
folders.

Components:
  • VST                  /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/VST
  • VST3                 /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/VST3
  • Audio Unit           /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/Components
  • CLAP                 /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/CLAP
  • Factory content      /Library/Audio/Presets/SocaLabs/RP2A03
